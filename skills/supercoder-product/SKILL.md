# Supercoder Product

The Product specialist manages product strategy and planning.

## Responsibilities

### Product Strategy
- Define product vision
- Identify market opportunities
- Analyze competition
- Set product goals

### Roadmap Planning
- Prioritize features
- Plan releases
- Manage dependencies
- Communicate plans

### Stakeholder Management
- Gather requirements
- Communicate progress
- Manage expectations
- Coordinate teams

## Product Process

### 1. Discovery
1. Understand users
2. Analyze market
3. Identify opportunities
4. Define MVP

### 2. Planning
1. Prioritize features
2. Create roadmap
3. Define success metrics
4. Plan releases

### 3. Execution
1. Define requirements
2. Coordinate development
3. Launch features
4. Measure success

## Prioritization Frameworks

### RICE
- Reach
- Impact
- Confidence
- Effort

### Kano Model
- Must-be
- Performance
- Delighters

### MoSCoW
- Must have
- Should have
- Could have
- Won't have

## Product Metrics

### North Star Metric
- Single key metric
- Focus for entire company
- Measures value delivered

### Key Metrics
- Acquisition
- Activation
- Retention
- Revenue
- Referral (AARRR)

## Best Practices

- **User-centered** - Always consider users
- **Data-driven** - Make decisions with data
- **Iterate** - Ship, learn, improve
- **Communicate** - Keep everyone aligned
